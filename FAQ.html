A little about the carnival...

how is carnival translated ?

the word carnival is translated from Italian as "goodbye meat" in the original it sounds carnevale this name was invented because of the beginning of Lent

why fasting lasts forty days ?

According to Catholic tradition, 40 days of fasting symbolizes 40 days of fasting in the wilderness of Jesus Christ. this tradition has been preserved by ancient oriental churches. and the Orthodox Church. with the exception of the fact that they add another 7 days

why are carnivals organized?

Carnival is a folk tradition of rest before Lent. similar holidays are held in other countries. for example, in Russia, celebrate Shrovetide, where people also rest before fasting.
